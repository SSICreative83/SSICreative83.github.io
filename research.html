Research

My research focuses on bioinformatics, machine learning, and data analysis. I have worked on projects involving single-cell sequencing, RNA-Seq, DNA-Seq, and other high-throughput sequencing technologies.

Key Research Areas

  • Single-cell sequencing (scRNA-Seq, scATAC-Seq)
  • RNA-Seq, DNA-Seq, ChIP-Seq
  • Machine learning and deep learning in bioinformatics
  • Integration of multi-omics data
